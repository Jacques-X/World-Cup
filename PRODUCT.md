# Product

## Register

product

## Users

A private group of five friends or family members making World Cup predictions from phones and desktop browsers. Participants primarily select their identity, enter scores quickly, compare the leaderboard, and inspect simulated group tables. One trusted admin manages names and official results.

## Product Purpose

Provide a dependable shared replacement for a browser-local prediction sheet. Success means every participant sees the same current data, can enter predictions without creating an account, and can understand scores and standings at a glance.

## Brand Personality

Competitive, direct, energetic.

## Anti-references

Avoid betting-site visual noise, decorative sports graphics, excessive animation, novelty controls, and generic SaaS dashboard styling.

## Design Principles

- Keep the active player unmistakable.
- Make score entry faster than spreadsheet entry.
- Let competition emerge from the data, not decoration.
- Keep admin power visible but separate from participant flow.
- Communicate every network save and failure clearly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast and keyboard operation. Never communicate scoring state with color alone. Respect reduced-motion preferences and keep touch targets usable on mobile.
